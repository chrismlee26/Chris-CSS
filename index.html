p. paragraph

				
					Try pairing different header elements together to provide quick context to your content.
					

					h1-h3 are primary headings.
					<h1>h1, a serif is the largest & is for primary categories</h1> 
					<h2>h2, sans-serif is the primary sub heading</h2>
					<h3>h3, sans-serif is the secondary sub heading. This font is evenly-spaced.</h3>
					

					h4-h6 are secondary subtitle fonts. Great for minor sections.
					<h4>h4, is a smaller grey h1</h4>
					<h5>h5, is a smaller grey h2</h5>
					<h6>h6, is a smaller grey h3</h6>
					

					
					<p>is for the main text<p>
